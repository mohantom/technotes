Route 53 (Managed DNS)
=========================

## In AWS, the most common records are:
- A: hostname to IPv4
- AAAA: hostname to IPv6
- CNAME: hostname to hostname
- Alias: hostname to AWS resource. 

## Routing policy
- Simple
- Weighted
- Latency
- Fail over
- Geolocation
